Privacy Policy

Kibo – Expense Tracker & Bill Splitter

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kibo – Expense Tracker & Bill Splitter ("Kibo", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

All financial data you enter in the App — including transactions, accounts, budgets, goals, shopping lists, and bill splits — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup, the App requests access to your Google Drive account (restricted to app-specific storage via the Drive App Data scope). Backup files are stored in a private folder only accessible to the App. We do not access, read, or share the content of your backups.

1.3 Crash and Diagnostic Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics (by Google) to automatically collect anonymous crash reports when the App encounters an error. This may include:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Anonymised crash stack traces

No personally identifiable information or financial data is included in crash reports.

1.4 Advertising Data (Free Users)

If you use the free version of the App, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android GAID)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information and approximate location

Ad personalisation is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and your device's ad preferences. You can opt out of personalised ads in your Android device settings under Google > Ads.

1.5 In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment details. We receive only confirmation of your subscription status (active/inactive) to unlock Premium features.

1.6 Exchange Rate Data

To display currency conversions, the App fetches exchange rate data from a public API. No personal information is sent in these requests.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and improve the core features of the App
  • Restore your data from Google Drive backups upon your request
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs via Crashlytics
  • Display relevant advertisements to free-tier users via AdMob
  • Verify your subscription status to unlock Premium features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. If you enable Google Drive Backup, your backup files are stored in your personal Google Drive under a private, app-restricted folder.

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including:

  • Local-only data storage by default
  • Optional biometric authentication to lock the App
  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS) for all network requests

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google (Firebase Crashlytics, AdMob, Google Drive, Google Play Billing) processes data as described above under their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

No financial data stored locally on your device is ever shared with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Drive API

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Portability: Your financial data lives on your device. You can export it at any time via the App's export feature.
  • Deletion: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App or using the "Delete all data" option in Settings.
  • Backup Deletion: You can delete your Google Drive backup at any time from your Google Drive account under Settings > Manage Apps.
  • Ad Preferences: You can opt out of personalised advertising in your Android device settings (Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation).
  • Crashlytics: You can disable crash reporting in the App's Settings menu.

8. Data Retention

Your financial data is retained on your device until you delete it. Crash logs collected by Firebase Crashlytics are retained by Google for up to 90 days. We do not independently store crash data beyond what Google's systems retain.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
